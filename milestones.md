Proposed Milestone Plan

Milestone 1: Foundation & Auth : Laravel scaffold, 5 roles (super_admin, general_manager, production_manager, store_manager, distributor), login, OTP password reset, role-based dashboards + sidebar — full design matching country-yoghurt


Milestone 2: Product & User Management : Super Admin creates master product list (Name, Category, SKU, Size/Volume, Packaging, Unit), creates managers & distributors, sets pricing & per-distributor discounts

Milestone 3: Production & Inventory : Production Manager uploads batches (qty, batch#, production date, expiry, remarks), edits unverified uploads; Store Manager verifies; notifications fire to relevant roles

Milestone 4: Dispatch & Distribution : Store Manager dispatches to distributors; dispatch history & stock movement; Distributor views received goods, invoices, payment history, balances; notifications on dispatch

Milestone 5: Audit, Deletion & Reports : Inventory clearance policy (force PDF download before delete, audit trail log), deleted inventory log, role-scoped reports with charts, full notification system
